About

I am a cognitive science student at the University of Tuebingen and write my bachelor thesis about 'AI planning using the Planning Domain Definition Language PDDL' at the Wilhelm-Schickard-Institut under the supervision of Alexandra Kirsch. Searching for an efficient way of writing PDDL domains and problems, I decided to create a PDDL package for the awesome Sublime Text Editor.

If you have any questions, issues or ideas, please feel free to contact me. My email address is:
volker.strobel87@gmail.com

Related work:

  • PDDL Studio by Miroslav Chomut and Tomas Plch - an IDE for developing PDDL documents
  • PDDL Mode by Surendra K Singhi - an extension for GNU Emacs that supports PDDL 2.2
